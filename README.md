# Trump Trillion Tracker — data pack

A prototype "web-of-cronyism" dashboard tracking the publicly reported business
stakes, crypto ventures, foreign money, gifts and government favors flowing to
the Trump–Kushner family — framed as a countdown to a $1,000,000,000,000 net worth.

**Open `trump-trillion-tracker.html` in any browser.** It's fully self-contained
(no build step, no internet needed) — animated net-worth odometer, per-member
cards, categorized revenue streams, an interactive force-directed network graph,
a "favor ledger" timeline, and a full sources list.

---

## ⚠️ Read this first (methodology & disclaimer)

This is a **journalism-aggregation prototype** built from public reporting
(Forbes, Bloomberg, WSJ, CNN, ABC, Reuters, CoinDesk, congressional disclosures,
and watchdog trackers). Dollar figures are **estimates** — many include volatile
or unrealized crypto/equity value — and reasonable sources disagree by billions.
Entries describe **reported conflicts of interest, not adjudicated wrongdoing.**
The "$1 trillion" goal is a satirical framing device, not a forecast.
Data current to ~June 2026.

Headline number anchors on Forbes' late-2025 estimate of the extended family's
combined fortune (~$10B), cross-checked against House Oversight Democrats' tally
of presidency-linked gains (up to $9.72B incl. paper wealth) and WSJ reporting
on World Liberty Financial.

---

## Why this didn't really exist before

The data already lives in trackers — CAP's "Trump's Take", CREW, Public Citizen,
and House Oversight's "Digital Grift Wealth Tracker". The gap is the **framing**:
nobody made it a single countdown to $1T *and* drew the network of who pays whom
and which favor came back. It's hard because the numbers are fuzzy and fast
(crypto swings daily; "net worth" mixes cash, equity, and paper gains; estimates
disagree by billions), so a live tracker needs a defensible methodology.

## Revenue streams

**Crypto**
- **World Liberty Financial (WLFI):** $1.4B+ to Trump & Witkoff families. WSJ: ≥$1.2B cash + ~$2.25B paper token gains over 16 months.
- **USD1 stablecoin:** $4.6B circulating (Apr 2026). Abu Dhabi's MGX routed a $2B Binance investment through USD1; est. $60–80M/yr yield to WLFI.
- **$TRUMP memecoin:** ~$320M creator fees (Chainalysis); 813,000+ wallets lost a combined ~$2B.
- **Trump Media (DJT):** ~$3.65B market cap; now a Bitcoin treasury (~$2.5B BTC). Majority owned by the Donald J. Trump Revocable Trust.
- **American Bitcoin (ABTC):** $5B valuation on Sept 2025 Nasdaq debut; sons own ~20% (w/ Hut 8).

**Foreign capital**
- **Kushner / Affinity Partners:** $6.16B AUM, ~99% foreign. Saudi PIF ~$2B (overruling its own screening panel) + $1.5B from Qatar QIA and Abu Dhabi Lunate (2024).
- **UAE stake in WLFI:** $500M reported (interests linked to Sheikh Tahnoon bin Zayed); under House probe.

**Real estate**
- **25** Trump-branded foreign projects during the term (CREW) — Qatar golf resort, Oman govt-land resort, UAE & Saudi towers.
- **LIV Golf** (Saudi PIF-backed) tournaments at Trump-owned U.S. courses (Doral, Bedminster).

**Gifts**
- **Qatar Boeing 747-8 jet:** ~$400M, accepted by the Pentagon (May 2025) as a future Air Force One; conversion could cost up to $1B.

**Merch / name-image-likeness**
- **~$600M** licensing income (financial disclosure): sneakers+fragrance $2.5M, watches $2.8M, Greenwood Bible $1.3M, "45" guitar $1.05M, NFT royalties $1.16M.
- **Trump Mobile / T1 phone:** $499 phone, $47.45/mo plan; license fees to the family trust.
