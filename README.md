# Trump Trillion Tracker

A self-contained HTML dashboard tracking publicly reported money flowing to the Trump–Kushner family. Open `trump-trillion-tracker.html` in any browser.

## ⚠️ Read this first (methodology & disclaimer)

Every dollar figure is an estimate aggregated from public reporting (Forbes, Bloomberg, WSJ, CNN, CoinDesk, congressional disclosures, and watchdog trackers). Many figures include unrealized crypto or equity value, and sources disagree by billions.

The headline number sums the largest available net-worth estimate for each family member (about $9.5B as of mid-2026). Kushner's card uses his ~$1B personal net worth, not the $6.16B his firm manages. Where a figure is a company valuation rather than cash, the page notes the family's ownership share. The wealth-over-time chart is a reconstruction calibrated to those endpoints, not a precise ledger.

Timeline and favor-ledger entries describe reported conflicts of interest, not adjudicated wrongdoing. The "$1 trillion" goal is satirical. Data current to ~June 2026.
